About Aesop

Aesop lived between 500 and 600 B.C. and was a contemporary of Solon and of Croesus, King of Lydia. Very little is known about him except that in the following century his Fables ahd a great reputation; they are noticed by Aristophanes and Plato... (Dr. Lowe)

The Cock and the Robbers
